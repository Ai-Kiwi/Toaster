Welcome to Toaster Community Guidelines

1. Spread Positivity

Encourage and uplift fellow Toaster users. Toast is about warmth, so let's keep our conversations warm and supportive.

2. Respect Diversity

Embrace and celebrate the diverse range of toast creations and cooking styles. Respect differences in culture, taste, and presentation.

3. Keep it Toast-Centric

Stick to toast-related content. Share pictures of your delicious toast creations, your recipes, and your toasting tips. This is not a platform for unrelated content.

4. Mind Your Manners

Be polite and respectful. Avoid hate speech, offensive language, and any form of harassment.

5. No Explicit Content

We're here for toast, not anything explicit or inappropriate. Please keep all content family-friendly.

6. Protect Your Privacy

Don't share personal information or engage in doxing (revealing personal details of others without consent). Respect privacy.

7. Intellectual Property

Respect copyright and intellectual property rights. Only post content that you have the rights to share.
